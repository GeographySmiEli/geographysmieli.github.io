Geography Club Poster Designs

Poster

During my tenure in the Geography Club at CSUN, I advanced from Secretary to Treasurer, Vice President, and finally President, each role sharpening my organizational and leadership skills. From coordinating events like workshops and guest speaker sessions to managing club finances, I honed my ability to lead our team and ensure the smooth execution of complex tasks alongside building and cultivating a sense of belonging and engagement between club members and department faculty.

Utilizing tools such as Google Forms and Google Sheets, I provided a space for member collaboration and brainstorming, analyzed events for turnout for future reference, and utilized the data for financing pitches towards the University Associated Student body for camping trips and conference trips. Due to my campaigning efforts, we were able to finance dozens of students per year to attend various Geography conferences in the US.

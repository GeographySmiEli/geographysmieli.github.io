CSUN Tree Atlus

Survey, Presentation, Award

For this project, I worked alongside 9 other students on behalf of the California State University Northridge Institute of Sustainability and the campus Physical Plant Management to provide comments, corrections and feedback for the campus tree atlas. I also presented our work to the Office of Community Engagement 2016 Annual Spring Research and Service Symposium. Alongside my partner, Justine Maybrun, we earned Third Place for our presentation and updated the community on our work done for the school.

This project had a profound impact on my interests in trees and their effects of mitigation of the urban heat island effect. I gained an appreciation for the work and consideration needed for field measurement and mapping in the field. By setting up a Google Sheets and Arc Survey123 utilizing the latest aerial imagery of the school, I identified and edited errors in the field about tree tag status, species names, dedications, and physical location.

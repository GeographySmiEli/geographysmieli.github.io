The One Ring Infographic

Infographic

As a bit of fun with an assignment on behalf of an Experimental Infographic class, this graphic is a timeline of the One Ring through the famous J.R.R Tolkien books. I enjoyed currating the typefaces and layout of the Ring as a time line using the start and ending point of the Ring at Mount Doom to display the timescales of its story.
